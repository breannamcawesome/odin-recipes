Pot Pie

Ingredients

  • 3 small potatoes
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup margarine
  • 2 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs flour
  • 2 bouillion cubes
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup corn
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1/2 cup green Beans
  • 1 yellow squash
  • 2 pie crust doughs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Peel potatoes. After rinsing, dice into small cubes. Boil in a medium saucepan in water until soft.
  3. After potatoes are soft, drain water. Mash potatoes with margarine. Add salt/pepper/seasonings to taste if desired.
  4. While potatoes are cooking, in a small saucepot, melt butter. Add flour, stir frequently until browned.
  5. Add bouilliom cubes and water, stir frequently until gravy is desired thickness.
  6. Stir in corn, peas, and green beans.
  7. Slice squash evenly.
  8. Carefully line pie dish with one pie crust dough. Fill the bottom of the pie with mashed potatoes.
  9. Place squash slices on top of the mashed potato layer, evenly covering.
  10. Pour in gravy and vegetable mixture.
  11. Cover top of pie with second dough, crimp around the edges. Cut out a few holes for ventilation on top.
  12. Bake in oven for about 20 minutes, or until crust is golden brown.
