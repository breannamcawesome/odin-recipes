Jell-O Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 3oz box of strawberry Jell-o
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 cup ice
  • 1 8oz containter of Cool Whip
  • 1 graham cracker pie crust

Steps

  1. Combine Jell-O powder with 1 cup boiling water. Stir until completely dissolved.
  2. Add 1 cup of ice to bowl. Stir until ice is melted.
  3. Refrigerate for 15 minutes.
  4. Stir in Cool Whip, until evenly mixed. Pie should be a consistent pastel pink color.
  5. Pour into graham cracker crust.
  6. Refrierate for 3 hours, or until completely set.
