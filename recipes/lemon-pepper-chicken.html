Lemon Pepper Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts, thawed
  • 1/3 cup lemon juice
  • 2tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • salt
  • lemon pepper seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. In a bowl, combine lemon juice, olive oil, and seasonings.
  3. Place chicken breasts in bowl. Massage them in the mixture, tenderizing gently. Let rest in bowl for 5 minutes.
  4. Place chicken breasts on a baking sheet.
  5. With a knife, slice several small lines in the top of the chicken breasts.
  6. Pour remaining mixture over the chicken breasts, working it into the slits on the top of the breasts.
  7. Bake in oven for 10-15 minutes, or until chicken is thoroughly cooked through.
